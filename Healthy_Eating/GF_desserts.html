Chocolate Avocado Mousse
Monday 16th November 2020

Delicious chocolate flavoured dessert made from fruit and cocoa powder.

Ingredients
  • Banana (ripe)
  • 4 Medjool dates (pitted)
  • 1 Avocado (ripe)
  • 3 tsp Cacao powder (add more depending on taste)

Method
  1. Add all the ingredients to a food processor and blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Taste and add more cocoa powder to suit taste.
